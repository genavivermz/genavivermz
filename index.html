Hello! I'm Genavive, an incoming college freshman from SoCal; I'm interested in studying computer science and STS. I love GIFs, tinkering with PCB design, listening to Miguel, and creating new flavor combos @ Dunkin'.

After growing up balancing 50+ hours a week of family responsibilities with my passion for technology, I decided to create WingedFounders.com: an acceleration platform built for constrained founders (AKA bedroom founders). The website is intended to help young social entrepreneurs bypass infrastructural barriers to scaling their efforts, ultimately aiming to reshape the current startup ecosystem into something more equitable. My invention is a 10-day sprint with different track options (raise goods, fundraise, grow a team, or gain recognition).
